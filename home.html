Welcome to Tehran Parks

Explore the beautiful parks and tourist attractions of Tehran, the bustling capital of Iran

Mellat Park is one of the largest and most popular parks in Tehran, perfect for relaxing, walking, and enjoying nature in the heart of the city

Located north of Tehran, Tochal Mountain offers stunning views, hiking trails, and a ski resort, making it a great spot for outdoor enthusiasts

Golestan Palace, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is one of the oldest historic monuments in Tehran, showcasing Persian art and architecture.

Tabiat Bridge is one of the architectural marvels of Tehran, connecting two major parks and offering beautiful views of the city
